Innovation Served Hotter Than Coffee: The Navchetna Story

Published on January 15, 2025 • Company Feature

Featured

From Five Friends to Innovation Leaders

Every great journey starts with a simple idea and ours started on 6th September 2024. Five friends Ayushmaan, Varun, Suman, Swastik, and Dhraval were sitting together, talking about school, college, and life, and suddenly realized something: education could be so much better with the right tools and technology.

From that conversation, late-night brainstorming sessions, and countless cups of coffee, Navchetna was born. Back then, we were just a bunch of dreamers with laptops and big ideas. No fancy offices, no investors, no roadmap just a shared vision and a lot of determination.

Company Milestone

Today, Navchetna is proud to be based in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India, and has grown into a company that delivers both innovative products and comprehensive services, with official AWS India collaboration ensuring enterprise-grade reliability.

Product Portfolio & Innovation

Our flagship products Osmium, Natraj, and Aegis auth are designed to make learning smarter and more accessible. Our services span everything from web, iOS, and Android development to AI/ML solutions, cybersecurity, and more. Basically, if it involves technology and innovation, we're on it.

Our Approach

  • • Plan – Listen, understand, and map out solutions
  • • Strategy – Clear steps and milestones
  • • Development – Build, tweak, and perfect
  • • Deployment – Launch, monitor, and support

Growth Journey

From five friends with a shared dream to a core team of six and a family of 15 passionate people, our growth has always been powered by curiosity, creativity, and a love for building things that matter.

Company Philosophy

"Navchetna isn't just a company. It's a story of friendship, late-night ideas, challenges, and small wins. It's about dreaming big, working hard, and believing that learning and innovation can truly change the world. And honestly? We're just getting started."

Innovation Fueled by Pure Adrenaline: Navchetna Recognized by DPIIT

We are thrilled to announce that Navchetna has been officially recognized by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Government of India. This recognition validates our commitment to innovation and positions us among India's most promising startups.

Official Recognition

The DPIIT recognition opens doors to various benefits including tax exemptions, easier compliance, faster patent examination, and access to government schemes. This milestone reflects our dedication to building innovative solutions that contribute to India's technological advancement.

What This Means for Us

This recognition is more than just a certificate. It's a testament to our team's hard work, our innovative approach, and our commitment to building world-class products. It validates our vision of creating technology that makes a real difference in education and beyond.

Tech Independence January 10, 2025

Building the Future on Our Own Terms: Navchetna Technologies is Now a Fully Independent Powerhouse

From Five Friends to Total Self Reliance. Every revolution starts with a spark and ours ignited on September 6, 2024. Five friends named Ayushmaan, Varun, Suman, Swastik, and Dhraval were deep in conversation about the state of technology. We realized that too many startups were just wrappers built on top of other peoples tools. We did not want to rent our success so we decided to build everything ourselves. Navchetna was born from the pure adrenaline of knowing we could engineer a complete ecosystem from the ground up without relying on anyone else.

The Engine: 100% Made in India with Our Own Tech Stack

Our transition from dreamers to industry leaders was powered by a bold decision to reject third party dependencies. We are proud to announce that Navchetna is now fully independent from top to bottom with our own suite of proprietary tools. We did not just build the NineLLMs solution. We built the entire infrastructure that supports it.

Security by Aegis Auth

We built our own fortress with Aegis Auth which features over 10 layers of security to ensure absolute data sovereignty. This is not just a concept because it is live and ready for developers right now. We have released our own packages for the entire ecosystem:

  • For JavaScript: npm i aegis-auth-navchetna
  • For web browsers: Direct CDN integration ready
  • For Python: pip install aegis-auth-navchetna
  • For Rust: cargo add aegis-auth-navchetna
  • For Flutter: aegis_auth
  • For Native Mobile: Android and iOS SDKs coming soon

Financial Integrity with NSL

We moved beyond simple billing and engineered NSL or Navchetna Smart Ledger. This is our proprietary ledger system built from scratch to maintain immutable financial records and ensure absolute transparency in every transaction.

Data Clarity with LMlens

From raw data extraction to deep analysis we utilize LMlens our proprietary extraction engine that feeds perfect data directly into our AI models.

Our Philosophy

Navchetna is not just a company. It is a declaration of independence. It is a story of friendship and the refusal to take shortcuts. It is about dreaming big and working fast while believing that true innovation means building the whole machine yourself. This means zero dependencies and zero black boxes. With Aegis Auth protecting the door, NSL managing the ledger, and LMlens processing the data, our technology is truly Made in India and built entirely in house. We own our tech and we own our future. And honestly we are just getting started.
